# Color System

## Brand Colors
- **Primary**: #FF3F6C (Vivid Raspberry) - Exclusive for CTAs, prices, and active states.
- **Secondary**: #282C3F (Midnight Slate) - Primary typography and icons.
- **Tertiary**: #535766 (Cool Pewter) - Secondary info, metadata, and borders.

## Surface Colors
- **Background**: #FAFBFC - Default page background.
- **Surface**: #FFFFFF - Product cards and containers.
- **Surface Variant**: #EAEAEC - Borders and subtle separators.

## Elevation
- **Low Elevation**: Soft ambient shadow (0px 4px 20px rgba(40, 44, 63, 0.05)).
- **High Elevation**: More pronounced shadow for modals/dropdowns.
