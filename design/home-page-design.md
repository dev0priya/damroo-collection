# Homepage Design Documentation

## 1. Hero Section (Slider/Banner)
- **Visuals**: High-resolution lifestyle imagery featuring festive wear.
- **Dynamic Fields**: 
  - `Title`: Catchy heading (e.g., "Elevate Your Style").
  - `Subtitle`: Supporting text (e.g., "Exquisite festive collections for every occasion").
  - `CTA`: Primary button (e.g., "Shop Now").
  - `Link`: Target URL.
  - `Image`: Optimized banner image.

## 2. Featured Categories
- **Layout**: Grid of 4-6 categories.
- **Visuals**: Circular or soft-rounded thumbnails.
- **Data Source**: CMS Categories marked as `active` and prioritized by `displayRank`.

## 3. Trending Products / Best Sellers
- **Layout**: 4-column product grid with "Trending" label.
- **Features**: 
  - Hover gallery (show second product image on hover).
  - Quick "Add to Cart" button.
  - Pricing with MRP and Sale Price.

## 4. About Us / Our Story (Snippet)
- **Layout**: Split view (Text on left, Image on right).
- **Goal**: Briefly communicate brand values and heritage.
- **Data Source**: Dynamic text and image from CMS settings.

## 5. Brand Promise (Icon Grid)
- **Items**:
  - **Free Shipping**: For orders above ₹999.
  - **Easy Returns**: 7-day hassle-free returns.
  - **Premium Quality**: Handcrafted with love.
  - **Secure Payment**: Trusted payment partners.

## 6. Newsletter Subscription
- **Visuals**: Minimalist design with a bold CTA button.
- **Behavior**: Real-time validation and success message.

## 7. Dynamic Footer
- **Sections**:
  - **Company Info**: About snippet, Address, Contact.
  - **Quick Links**: Dynamic Header/Footer menus.
  - **Policies**: Links to Page records (Terms, Privacy, etc.).
  - **Social Links**: Dynamic icons for Instagram, Facebook, Twitter.
  - **Copyright**: Dynamic year and brand name.
